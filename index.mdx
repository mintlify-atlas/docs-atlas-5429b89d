## Why NSD?

NSD is designed with a pure philosophy that prioritizes raw performance. If you serve hundreds of thousands or even millions of queries per second, NSD is the leading implementation in the world.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Extreme Performance" icon="gauge-high">

  <Card title="DNSSEC Ready" icon="shield-check">
    Full DNSSEC support with efficient signing and validation

  <Card title="Battle-Tested" icon="check-double">

## Production-Ready

NSD has been developed for operations in environments where speed, reliability, stability, and security are of high importance. It serves as the authoritative DNS server for numerous top-level domains and large-scale DNS infrastructures worldwide.

  NSD is distributed free of charge under the BSD-3-Clause license. For most platforms, [packages are available](https://repology.org/project/nsd/versions) through your system's package manager.
</Note>
